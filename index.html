INTP -- Logician

Overview

People with the Logician personality type view the world as a big, complex machine, and recognize that as with any machine, all parts are interrelated.

Strengths

Original, open-minded, objective

Weaknesses

Insensitive, absent-minded,contempt for authority, doubtful

Workplace Habits

The running theme for Logicians is their desire for solitude, need for intellectual stimulation, and the satisfaction of the final piece of a puzzle clicking into place.

Though Logicians may scoff at the notion, they actually function best when paired with another person.
